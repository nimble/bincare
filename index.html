Our Process

1

Step 1

The day after your garbage day, leave your bins at the end of your driveway and we will arrive between 7am and 6 pm.

2

Step 2

Upon arrival, we will begin the process of sanitizing and deodorizing your bins curb-side.

3

Step 3

Our custom built cleaning equipment collects all the grey water to be disposed of at a designated sani-dump.

4

Step 4

Your bins have now been sanitized with 200 degree water and 3000 psi killing 99% of dangerous germs and bacteria.

5

Step 5

Your bins receive a final inspection and an eco-friendly odor treatment before we return it to the curb-side where it was picked up.

About BinCare.ca

BinCare.ca is a professional bin cleaning service dedicated to keeping your community clean and odor-free. We use state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly processes to sanitize and deodorize your garbage bins.

Our high-temperature pressure washing system (200°F water at 3000 PSI) kills 99% of dangerous germs and bacteria, while our eco-friendly odor treatment keeps your bins fresh between cleanings.

We're committed to providing flexible, cost-effective service plans that fit your needs, with no contracts required. Serving the Tri-City, Ontario area.
